## Sample Policy Structure

This copilot works with any NBFC credit policy document in .docx format.

Your policy document should contain sections like:
- Eligible Customer Profile (age, income criteria)
- Credit Bureau Policy (minimum scores, rejection triggers)
- FOIR Policy (maximum FOIR by segment)
- KYC & AML Requirements
- Product Overview (loan amount, tenure, rates)

Add your policy .docx to the project folder and update 
POLICY_FILE in copilot.py to match the filename.
